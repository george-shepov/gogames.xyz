How to Play

1 · Bid

Predict how many of 13 tricks you'll win. Partners combine bids.

2 · Follow Suit

Play the led suit if you have it. Otherwise, discard or trump.

3 · Trump ♠

Spades beat any suit. Can't lead spades until broken.

4 · Score

Make bid = +10/trick + bags. Miss = −10/trick. Nil = ±100.

5 · Bags

Every 10 accumulated bags costs −100. First to 250 wins.

6 · Nil

Bid 0 tricks. Succeed = +100. Fail = −100.
